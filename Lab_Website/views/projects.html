Lyric Book

This book was created using Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom in my "ATLS 2100: Image" class. The book was created as a way for me to express myself and how I feel. Each image in the book goes along with a lyric from the song "This is Me Trying." This song resonates with me as it feels like sometimes I'm pushed to be perfect when in reality, I'm just trying my best.

Ease App

As my final project in "ATLS 1100: Design Foundations", I created an app called Ease using Adobe XD. The purpose of this app was to give resources to help others with mental health and give them ways to destress. The app includes 4 different tab: Journal, Stress Relief, Exercises, and Resources. The journal tab allows them to write down their thought and feeling and just express themselves. The stress relief tab allows them to watch some relaxing/satisfying videos. The exercises tab gives them some helpful breathing exercises to destress. Lastly, the resources tab provides mental health resources that they can call or contact if in need of help.
