Interests

Coding

I enjoy coding because it almost is like a puzzle, and when you get it right, it is the most satisfying feeling in the world. Seeing what I can create through code and discovering new possibilities is amazing and such a fun experience.

Acting

Acting allows me to use my creativity to bring to life an imaginary character. I get to escape into my own world and literally put myself into someone else's shoes. It a very cool experience and I enjoy every bit of it.

Music

I enjoy music not only to listen to, but to play as well. It allows me to escape my own world and discover the true meaning of different pieces of work. There is a lot that goes into creating music and it is so interesting to learn about the different ways artist convey their message.
